Llama 3 on Groq

Why? Ultra-low latency. We need near-instant voice conversations. Standard GPT-4o is great, but Groq LPUs give us the speed of talk.

Bhashini

Why? It's the gold standard for Indian languages. Unlike generic models, Bhashini handles rural dialects and accents effectively.

Firecrawl

Why? Gov portals are messy. Firecrawl navigates complex DOM structures to scrape clean data for our RAG pipeline.

FastAPI + Supabase

Why? Async capabilities for handling concurrent voice streams and a scalable, real-time database.
